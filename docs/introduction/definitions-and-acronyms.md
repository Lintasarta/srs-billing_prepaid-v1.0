# Definitions and Acronyms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| PPU | Pay Per Use. A billing method where customers are charged based on their actual usage, typically rounded up to the nearest hour. |
| Monthly | A billing method where services are charged monthly, with the daily charge calculated based on the number of days in the current month. |
| SME | Small Medium Enterprises |
| Daily Charging | The process of debiting the customer's deposit for services used occurring daily at 00:01 GMT+7. |
| Service Portal | The primary web interface for customers to manage their services and billing. |
| Superadmin | An administrative user with the highest level of permissions in the Service Portal, capable of managing pricing and system configurations. |
| Useradmin | A standard customer user role with permissions to manage services within their organization. |
| Deposit | The prepaid balance is held by a customer in their account to pay for services. |
| Ongoing Charging | The running total of charges accrued within the current billing period before the daily debit. |
